# The Pragmatic Home Baker's Manifesto

The pragmatic home baker knows a recipe is not enough. Dough is alive. Flour,
water, time, yeast, temperature: everything matters.

The pragmatic home baker sometimes plans the pizza two days ahead, and sometimes
decides in the middle of the day that tonight is the night.

The pragmatic home baker is not a slave to the dough. They fit the recipe to
their schedule, never their schedule to the dough.

The pragmatic home baker does not like to play dice. A good pizza is not luck,
it's numbers and technique.

Panetti is the tool for the pragmatic home baker.

## What Panetti is

A dough tool built on baker's percentages. Pick a style, dial in hydration,
salt, yeast and oil, and Panetti scales the exact gram weights to any number of
*panetti* — whether you're mixing tonight or building a poolish, biga or
sourdough two days out. The ferment guide and method card bend the recipe to
*your* schedule, not the other way around.
